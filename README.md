## ⚡ What is Medhaverse?

**Medhaverse** is a web-based city-building adventure game designed for students aged **10–14**. Players build a fully AI-powered smart city — but only by earning credits through solving real engineering puzzles first.

When the city is thriving and everything feels perfect — their own AI goes rogue. The city they built starts breaking apart. Now they must think, investigate, and rebuild like a real engineer.

**Every concept is taught 100% through gameplay. Zero lectures. Zero textbooks. Zero boring pop-ups.**

## 💥 The Crisis

<img src="https://readme-typing-svg.demolab.com?font=Fira+Code&size=15&duration=1500&pause=400&color=E53935&center=false&width=700&lines=Everything+is+green.+The+city+is+thriving...;Then+—+SPARK+goes+quiet.;Traffic+freezes.+Neighborhoods+go+dark.;The+AI+is+doing+exactly+what+YOU+told+it+to+do.;Time+to+enter+DEBUG+MODE." alt="Crisis" />

The crisis isn't random. It's a consequence. Of your own decisions. Of rules you wrote. Of things you forgot to think about.

Now enter **Debug Mode** — investigate your own logic, find the bias you created, fix the routing failure you caused, and rebuild everything smarter than before.

## 🏆 About This Project

Medhaverse is an entry for the **IEEE "Engineer a Spark" Competition** — a challenge to create imaginative, accessible resources that get students excited about engineering and technology.

**Theme:** Learning Through Consequence.
**Goal:** Make a 12-year-old feel like a real engineer — not by teaching them, but by letting them build something, break it with their own decisions, and find the strength to fix it.
